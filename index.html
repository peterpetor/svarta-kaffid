Our Story

A simple idea,
perfected over years

What started as a humble idea — hearty soup ladled into freshly baked bread — became one of Reykjavík's most beloved spots. We bake our bread fresh every morning, craft two unique soups daily, and serve them in the only bowl you'll ever want to eat.

No frills, no fuss. Just warmth, flavor, and the kind of comfort that makes you forget about the Icelandic wind outside.

Today's Menu

Two soup types. One unforgettable bowl.

Our menu changes daily, but the concept stays the same — rich, handcrafted soup served inside a warm, crusty bread bowl. Every bite is edible.

🥩
Hearty & Rich

The Meat Soup

Our signature lamb or beef soup — slow-simmered with root vegetables and Icelandic herbs, served in a freshly baked bread bowl with a side of butter. Rich, comforting, unforgettable.

🌿
Fresh & Vibrant

The Veggie Soup

A daily-changing vegetarian creation — from curry-ginger vegetable blends to creamy garden soups. Always plant-based, always packed with flavor, always in a bread bowl.

Menu rotates daily · Refills available · Coffee & espresso also served
